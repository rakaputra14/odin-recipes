Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish featuring breaded chicken cutlets fried until golden, then topped with rich tomato sauce and melted cheese. The contrast between the crispy exterior, tender chicken, and savory sauce creates a comforting, hearty meal that pairs perfectly with pasta or crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce or marinara
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Italian seasoning or dried herbs

Steps

  1. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness
  2. Coat the chicken in flour, egg, then bread crumbs
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the chicken until golden
  4. Place the fried chicken in a baking dish
  5. Spoon tomato sauce over each piece
  6. Add mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top
  7. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly
  8. Serve hot with pasta or salad
