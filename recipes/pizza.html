Pizza

Pizza is a flatbread dish originating from Italy, consisting of a crisp yet chewy dough base topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of savory toppings. Its balance of crunch, softness, and rich flavors makes it endlessly customizable while remaining a comforting, universally loved meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (flour, yeast, water, salt, olive oil)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh basil or oregano
  • Pepperoni, sausage, or other toppings (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top
  5. Add desired toppings and season lightly
  6. Bake the pizza until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
  7. Remove from the oven and drizzle with olive oil
  8. Slice and serve hot
